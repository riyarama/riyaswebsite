Anne of Green Gables

Anne of Green Gables tells the heartwarming story of an orphan in Nova Scotia. Anne of Green Gables is a heartwarming story of growing up and acceptance, especially as a young lady in a late victorian society. It is a wonderful read and puts a lot into perspective.
