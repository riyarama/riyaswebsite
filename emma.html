Emma

Emma by Jane Austen tells the story of a young woman named Emma navigating her way through Victorian courting. Time and time again Emma is in the pretense of doing right but, she actually interprates all of her situations in the wrong way. Emma is a clever story about inexperience and the journies of youth, it is one of my favorite novels of all time!
