The Strange Case of Dr. Jeckyll and Mr. Hyde

The Strange Case of Dr. Jeckyll and Mr. Hyde tells the perplexing mystery behind the good Dr. Jeckyll and the infamous Mr. Hyde. Dr.Jeckyll mysteriously disappears during the night while the devious Mr.Hyde becomes inactive during the morning. It is up to the town to solve the case, this compelling mysetry explores gothic themes of the supernatural and ability of the human mind.
