Alice in Wonderland

Alice in Wonderland is a classic children's novel written by Lewis Carroll about the misadventures of Alice in the fantastical realm of Wonderland. The book resides deep in my heart as it was one of my childhood favorites and still continues to be with it's fantastic whimsy and quirky rules.
