Wuthering Heights

Wuthering Heights is a classic late victorian novel written by Emily Bronte, it fits the gothic romantic victorian genre with compelling characters such as Catherine Earnshaw, Heathcliff and Lockwood. I would give this book 5 stars because of it's dedicatioon to the gothic genre and it's compelling character dynamics.
